## What is the project?

Solar panels will be installed on an existing commercial rooftop to generate clean, renewable electricity. This project makes use of underutilized rooftop space and supports Quebec's transition to sustainable energy. It is being developed as part of Hydro-Québec's 2025-01 call for proposals for solar energy.

## What you will learn at this session

- How solar energy works and its benefits for the local electrical grid
- How existing, underutilized rooftops can become a valuable resource
- What to expect during construction and ongoing operations
- Quebec's renewable energy goals and available programs
- How you can participate in the program and potentially reduce your own building operating costs
