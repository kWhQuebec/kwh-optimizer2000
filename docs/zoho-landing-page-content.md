## DESIGN NOTES FOR ZOHO

### Colors (Copy these hex values)
- Primary Blue: #003DA6
- Accent Gold: #FFB005
- Success Green: #22C55E
- Warning Amber: #F59E0B
- Background Light: #F5F5F5
- Text Dark: #333333

### Font Stack
- Headings: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Body: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif

### Layout Recommendations
1. Single column above the fold
2. Form on right side (desktop) or below content (mobile)
3. Trust badges near form
4. Social proof / testimonials if available
5. Sticky CTA button on mobile

### Images to Include
- Hero: Commercial building with solar panels
- Benefits: Icons for each benefit
- Trust: Partner logos (Hydro-Québec, Rematek Energy)
- Background: Subtle gradient or Quebec imagery

## NEXT STEPS AFTER LAUNCH

1. **Week 1-2:** Collect baseline data
2. **Week 3-4:** Start A/B tests on headlines
3. **Week 5-6:** Optimize form fields based on completion rates
4. **Week 7-8:** Test CTA variations
5. **Monthly:** Review conversion rates and adjust messaging

### Success Metrics
- Target conversion rate: 3-5% (cold traffic), 10-15% (warm traffic)
- Cost per lead target: < $50 CAD
- Lead to opportunity rate: > 20%
- Opportunity to close rate: > 15%
